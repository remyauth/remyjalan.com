--- layout: page title: About permalink: /about/ priority: 0.9 intro: Welcome! I’m an author who mostly self-publishes what he writes in his spare time. Over the past few years, I’ve released three entries in my Elemental gay-lit series. Smolder started my journey as an author in 2017, with Bethesda following in 2019. I released Chances as Elemental’s third installment in late 2022. I live in Durham, North Carolina with my partner of more than sixteen years and our cat, Finn. By day, I work in academic research as a biomedical engineer. The self-publishing world is a big space and I’m excited to be here, despite being relatively unknown. I am a slow writer, but I try to create the best experience I can in whatever world I’ve discovered along the way. Feel free to contact me on social media, and if you’ve read my work, please leave a good review on sites like Amazon or GoodReads. Your words can amplify the number of people I reach.
